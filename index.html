Loan Process

Simple 3-Step Process

No paperwork. No hidden charges. A fair, community-backed loan system.

01
💰
Save Regularly
Members contribute a fixed amount every month to the common fund. Your savings build your eligibility.
02
📋
Request a Loan
Apply to the committee with your requirement. Approved based on savings tenure and contribution.
03
📅
Repay in EMIs
Repay in 12 equal monthly instalments. Track every payment through your personal member portal.
